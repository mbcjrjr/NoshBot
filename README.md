Brought to you by the Meateorologists


Made with the assistence of Gemini 

Website: noshbot.netlify.app

NoshBot is a smart, responsive Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help William & Mary students navigate campus dining. It allows users to filter menus based on specific dietary restrictions (Allergies, Vegan, Halal, Kosher), plan their weekly meals, and receive AI-powered nutritional advice.

Features

For Students

- Dietary Profiling: Filter the entire menu based on Lifestyle (Vegan, Vegetarian) and Allergies (Gluten, Dairy, Nuts, Soy, Shellfish).

- Dietary Laws: Specific support for Halal and Kosher requirements.

- Weekly Planner: A visual 7-day calendar to plan Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

- Smart Menu: Only shows food items safe for your specific profile.

- AI Nutritionist: Integrated Google Gemini AI analyzes your weekly plan and offers suggestions to balance your diet.

- Dark Mode: Toggle between W&M Green/Gold (Light) and Dark Mode.

- PWA Support: Installable on mobile devices with offline capabilities for viewing schedules without internet.

For Admins
- Menu Management: Add items with specific stations (Global Kitchen, True Balance, etc.) and dietary tags.

- Bulk Upload: Import massive JSON lists of food items in seconds.

- Recurring Items: Easily set "Staple" items (like eggs or salad bar) to appear every day of the week automatically.

  

Tech Stack
- Frontend: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).

- Styling: Tailwind CSS (via CDN).

- Database: Google Firebase Firestore.

- Authentication: Firebase Google Auth.

- AI: Google Gemini API.

- PWA: Service Workers & Web Manifest.
